# Email Safety Policy

- Future first cold email drafts should be 140 words or fewer.
- Sender identity must be clear.
- A working unsubscribe link is required when sending is implemented.
- Business-specific observations must be evidence-based.
- Do not claim a business has no website unless at least two future verification sources support it.
- Avoid spammy terms such as guaranteed, 100%, free money, and limited-time pressure.
- Do not imply a prior relationship.
- Pilot emails must not send without manual approval.
