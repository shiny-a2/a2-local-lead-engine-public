# Rewrite Brief Policy

Rewrite briefs are structured guidance for Phase 7 or a later human editor. Phase 8 must not generate a new email body, subject line, followup, or final copy.

A rewrite brief may include:
- why the draft failed
- what must be kept
- what must be removed
- what must be softened
- what evidence or claim mapping is missing

Unsafe claims, aggressive economic wording, missing unsubscribe placeholders, missing sender identity, multiple CTAs, and unsupported evidence should be listed in `must_remove` or `must_soften`.

Rewrite briefs do not authorize sending.
