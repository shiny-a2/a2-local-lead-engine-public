# Future Copy Evidence Guard

Future email must be natural, helpful, and non-promotional. Phase 3 consolidates evidence only;
it does not generate email copy.

Safe Phase 3 future-copy evidence types are business name, category hint, suburb hint, local area
hint, and service category hint.

Risky evidence includes website missing, raw email, raw phone, opening hours, and NZBN legal
details. Website absence cannot be claimed yet. Raw email and phone do not grant outreach
permission.
