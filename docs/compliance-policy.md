# Compliance Policy

- MVP outreach may use only business or generic emails.
- Personal email outreach is not allowed in the MVP.
- Future sending must include unsubscribe handling.
- Suppression list checks are required before sending.
- No repeated outreach after unsubscribe, bounce, complaint, or manual block.
- No false claims, fabricated observations, or implied prior relationship.
- Google Maps/Places usage is prohibited in the MVP.
- Voice calls are not part of the MVP.
- Manual approval is required for first pilot sends.
- Max daily send default is 10.
