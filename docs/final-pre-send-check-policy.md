# Final Pre-Send Check Policy

Required gates:
- sender identity
- unsubscribe placeholder
- recipient contact
- suppression
- claim safety
- body length
- subject safety
- single CTA
- judge validity
- staleness
- manual approval
- mailbox readiness

Suppression failures, unsafe claims, missing unsubscribe placeholder, missing sender identity, unsafe contact, multiple CTAs, invalid judge state, and major edits requiring rejudge block Phase 10 readiness.
