# Data Source Policy

- Google Maps and Google Places are prohibited in the MVP.
- Geoapify may be added in future Phase 2.
- OSM/Overpass may be added in future Phase 2 with rate limits and caching.
- NZBN may be added for enrichment in future Phase 2 or Phase 4.
- Tavily may be added for verification in future Phase 4.
- No single source is enough to claim that a business has no website.
- Raw payload storage and stable source fingerprints are required.
- Rate limiting and caching are required in connector phases.
