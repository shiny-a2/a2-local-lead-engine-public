# Candidate Quality Policy

Candidate quality is data readiness, not sales lead scoring.

Readiness thresholds:

- 80-100: ready for Phase 4
- 65-79: ready for Phase 4 with warnings
- 50-64: manual review
- below 50: needs more data or reject

Phase 4 readiness requires usable name, canonical category, city/country, location evidence,
source provenance, and acceptable duplicate/chain risk.
