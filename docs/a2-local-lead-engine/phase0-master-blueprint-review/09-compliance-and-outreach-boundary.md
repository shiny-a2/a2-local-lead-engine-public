# Compliance And Outreach Boundary

This project does not assume legal compliance is solved by code. Manual legal/compliance review is required before live outreach.

Rules:
- MVP uses generic/role business emails only.
- Personal/risky emails are blocked or manual review only.
- Unsubscribe is mandatory before any real send.
- Suppression is mandatory and must include manual blocks, unsubscribes, bounces, and legal risk.
- Claims must be truthful, evidence-bound, and permissioned.
- No absolute “you do not have a website” claim.
- No guaranteed bookings, savings, commission elimination, or platform attacks.
- No Google Maps scraping or Places usage in MVP.
- No automatic pricing, meeting scheduling, proposal sending, payment links, replies, or follow-ups.
- Human control points exist at draft approval, final pre-send review, sales response, pricing, proposals, and scale decisions.

فارسی: سیستم فقط کمک تصمیم‌گیری و کنترل عملیاتی می‌دهد؛ تصمیم فروش و ارتباط واقعی با مشتری باید انسانی بماند.
